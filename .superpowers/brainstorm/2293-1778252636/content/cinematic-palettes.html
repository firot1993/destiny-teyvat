Keep parchment-adjacent (cooler bone, less yellow), but earn it with a single saturated accent — deep vermilion ink, the kind from a wax seal. Used only on the eyebrow, the selected underline, and the scroll hint. The cream becomes intentional because of what punctuates it.

Per-character/per-Vision tinting still works on top of any of these — Pyro might warm the midnight ground toward ember, Cryo cool the bone toward pale ice, etc. The base palette sets the voice; the Vision is the seasoning.
